About UdharKhata Plus

UdharKhata Plus is India's most trusted digital credit ledger application, designed specifically for small and medium businesses. We understand the challenges faced by kirana shops, wholesalers, and B2B distributors in managing customer credit and tracking daily transactions.

Our mission is to empower Indian businesses with simple, powerful, and affordable technology. We believe that every shopkeeper deserves access to tools that make their work easier, their business more organized, and their growth faster.

Built with offline-first architecture, voice input in regional languages, and seamless UPI integration, UdharKhata Plus is the complete khata solution that works the way you work.
